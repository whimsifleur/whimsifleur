whimsifleur

It all started with my wedding bouquet - a lush red bouquet that I made the night before. I handpicked each bloom from the flower market and hand tied her with ribbons I picked up from a random quaint little shop in Prague.

I love flowers! I especially love quirky bouquets who have a bit of character. I couldn’t decide between a colourful bouquet or a single colour tone bouquet and that’s how I ended up making my own! Indecisive till the very last minute!

I always thought I wanted a huge wild unruly bouquet for my wedding, but when I saw this weird velvety textured wavy flower, I knew I had to have her in my bouquet. And that’s how I ended up making a cute petite bouquet with her as the star of the show. I found out later that her name is Celosia, and she symbolizes affection, singularity and silliness, how very apt!

Fast forward to couple of months after the wedding, I still love flowers and itched to make bouquets, but had no occasion (nor resources! Pretty things come with a pretty price tag!)...hence the inception of whimsifleur.

I hope you’ll feel the fun and joy in your bouquet, because that’s how I felt making her for you. Thank you, for trusting me to curate her for you. ❤️
