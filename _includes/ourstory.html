Our Story

whimsifleur is a small home-based business, aiming to provide clients with bespoke bouquets curated with a generous dose of t.l.c. and a touch of whimsy. At whimsifleur, every bouquet that goes out is thoughtfully and meticulously curated - we derive most joy when our clients love our works!

A chat with the Co-Founder of whimsifleur on how it all began:

"It all started with my wedding bouquet - a lush red bouquet that I made the night before. I handpicked each bloom from the flower market and hand-tied her with ribbons that I picked up from a random quaint little shop in Prague, souvenirs from past travels.

I adore flowers! Especially quirky bouquets who have a bit of character. I couldn’t decide between a colourful bouquet or a single-colour toned bouquet and that’s how I ended up making my own! I was so indecisive till the very last minute, I was afraid no florist would take my order!

I always thought that I wanted a huge wild unruly bouquet for my wedding, but when I saw this weird velvety textured wavy flower, I knew I had to have her in my bouquet. And that’s how I ended up making a cute petite bouquet with her as the star of the show. I found out later that her name is Celosia, and she symbolizes affection, singularity and silliness, how very apt!

Fast forward to couple of months after the wedding, I still love flowers and itched to make bouquets, but had no occasion (nor resources!)...hence the inception of whimsifleur.

I hope you’ll feel the fun and joy in your bouquet, because that’s how I feel making her for you. Thank you, for trusting us at whimsifleur to curate her for you. ❤️"
